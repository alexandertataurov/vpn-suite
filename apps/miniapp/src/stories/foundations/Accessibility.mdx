## Why it exists

Accessible design ensures the app works for everyone: keyboard users, screen readers, low vision, and users who prefer reduced motion.

## Rules

- **Color contrast:** Text/background pairs must meet WCAG AA (4.5:1 body, 3:1 large)
- **Focus visibility:** Use `--color-focus-ring`; never remove focus outlines
- **Touch targets:** Minimum 48×48px for interactive elements
- **Motion:** Respect `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce`; avoid auto-playing animations
- **Semantics:** Use correct HTML elements; labels for form controls

## Color contrast

| Pair | Minimum ratio |
|------|---------------|
| Body text on background | 4.5:1 |
| Large text (18px+) on background | 3:1 |
| UI components | 3:1 |

## Focus

- Focus ring: `var(--focus-ring)` or `var(--shadow-focus)`
- Visible on all interactive elements
- Never use `outline: none` without a replacement

## Touch targets

- Minimum: `--size-touch-target` (48px)
- Spacing between targets: at least 8px

## Reduced motion

The design system applies `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce` in `base.css` to shorten animations. Ensure critical information is not conveyed by motion alone.
