## Why it exists

Shadows communicate elevation and hierarchy. Semantic shadow tokens ensure consistent depth across surfaces and focus states.

## Rules

- Use `--shadow-*` tokens for elevation
- Do not hardcode box-shadow values
- Focus ring: use `--shadow-focus` or `--color-focus-ring` for focus visibility

## Implementation notes

- Tokens: `--shadow-sm`, `--shadow-md`, `--shadow-lg`, `--shadow-focus`
- Layering: higher elevation = stronger shadow

## Accessibility

- Focus outline must be visible; use `--shadow-focus` or equivalent
