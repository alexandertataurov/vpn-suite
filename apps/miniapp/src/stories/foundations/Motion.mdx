## Hero rationale

Consistent motion creates predictable, polished interactions. Duration and easing tokens keep animations aligned across the app.

## Rules

- Use `--duration-*` and `--ease-*` tokens
- Do not hardcode transition durations
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce` for accessibility

## Do / Don't

<DoDont
  do={<span>Use duration + easing tokens (`--duration-*`, `--ease-*`) for consistent feel</span>}
  dont={<span>Hardcode random `transition: 123ms` values</span>}

## Implementation notes

- Duration: `--duration-fast`, `--duration-normal`, `--duration-slow`
- Easing: `--ease-in`, `--ease-out`, `--ease-in-out`

## Accessibility

- The design system shortens animations when `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce` is set
- Do not convey critical information through motion alone
